What it does

Every feature you need.
Nothing you don't.

DayDrop lives entirely in your menu bar. It's always there when you want it, invisible when you don't.

Daily auto-refresh

Checks for a new photo every hour. When a fresh one drops, it downloads and applies it automatically.

Browse history

The last 8 daily photos are always one click away. Pick any and it sets instantly.

All screens covered

Sets the wallpaper on every connected monitor simultaneously, no manual juggling.

12 global regions

Choose a region and see the daily photo curated for that market — different locations, different images.

Zero dependencies

Pure Swift. No CocoaPods, no third-party frameworks. Just AppKit, SwiftUI, and the system APIs.

Sandboxed & private

Runs in the macOS App Sandbox. No telemetry, no tracking. Downloads images from Bing and nothing else.
